Direction

In my opinion, deep understanding of the brief and client is a must in the starting point. Determining project goals and functional specifications, knowing competitions and target audiences, making researches will be taken at this stage in order to find a precise combination between Typography, Color, Composition, Motion and brand assets.

Design

After collecting a right mood and tone in the Direction stage. My mission at this point is connecting the dots and making various versions to find the proper design. My goal is always about creating an experience that not only emphasises the characteristics of each project but also elegant and satisfied to use.
